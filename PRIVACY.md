# Privacy Policy for Lasker Chrome Extension

**Last updated: May 2026**

Lasker ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension.

## 1. No Data Collection
Lasker is designed with privacy as a priority. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII), browsing history, or chess game data to external servers. 

## 2. How the Extension Works
All chess analysis is performed locally within your browser or via established chess platform APIs. The "host permissions" requested by the extension are used solely to:
* Detect the current board state on supported chess websites.
* Provide real-time analysis and move suggestions.

## 3. Data Storage
The extension uses Chrome's `storage` API to save your personal preferences (such as engine depth or UI themes) locally on your device. This data is never uploaded to any cloud service or shared with third parties.

## 4. No Third-Party Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. Since we do not collect any data, there is no data to share.

## 5. Compliance
This extension complies with the Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies, including the "Limited Use" requirements.

## Contact
If you have any questions regarding this policy, you can open an issue on our GitHub repository.
